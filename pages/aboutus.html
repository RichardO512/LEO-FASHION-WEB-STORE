The Brand

What started as a mere idea was made into reality by three 1st year college students. Launched in 2025, L.E.O is one of the rising leading brands focused on casual everyday clothing, ensuring to deliver high-quality items for everyone while staying on-top of the current fashion trends. We at L.E.O strive to give only the best for our customers—by prioritizing comfort while while providing versatile and timeless pieces that will be with you every step of your journey.


Our Story

The brand's name, 'L.E.O', was combined from the first letters of the three 1st year college students' surnames, of which all three majored in Information Technology in Arellano University. What initially started as a group project, eventually evolved into a startup, and finally a full-fledged company with 500+ employees and 20+ branches across the metro. In 2026, L.E.O continues to grow, with more loyal customers joining the family and shopping for timeless clothing to their heart's content.
